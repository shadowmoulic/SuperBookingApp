Yeah, and honestly Roamy is exactly the type of app you should be studying right now.

Their core value proposition isn't:

Book attractions

It's:

Save places → organize them → generate itinerary.

They let users import places from Instagram, TikTok, screenshots, Google Maps links, etc., then automatically build optimized day-by-day itineraries and map routes.

The Important Part

For Zeque, don't copy Roamy.

Copy the workflow.

Roamy's flow:

Saved Places
     ↓
Map
     ↓
AI Itinerary
     ↓
Trip

Your flow should be:

Location
     ↓
Discover Attractions
     ↓
AI Itinerary
     ↓
Book Tickets

Notice the difference?

Roamy ends at itinerary.

You end at revenue.
