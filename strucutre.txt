### Final URL Structure I'd commit to

```text
/rajasthan

/jaipur

/jaipur/amer-fort

/category/forts

/trail/golden-triangle

/jaipur/forts

/jaipur/museums

/jaipur/2-day-itinerary
```

I would **not** use:

```text
/ state / rajasthan

/ city / jaipur
```

Google doesn't need it, users don't need it, and shorter URLs are cleaner.
